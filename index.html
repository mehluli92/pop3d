Materials That We Offer

ABS

Acrylonitrile butadiene styrene(ABS) is a common thermoplastic used to make light, rigid, molded products such as automotive body parts, wheel covers and protective head gear

PLA

Polylactic Acid, commonly known as PLA, is one of the most popular materials used in desktop 3D printing. It is the default filament of choice for most extrusion-based 3D printers.

Standard Resin

Resin is a liquid that turns into plastic through a chemical reaction with UV light.
